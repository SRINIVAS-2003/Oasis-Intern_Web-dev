A.P.J.Abdul Kalam

Indian Scientist and Politician leader

 

Here are some points about Dr.Kalam

  • His full name is Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam.
  • Dr. A. P.J. Abdul Kalam is also known as Missile Man.
  • He was born on15 October 1931.
  • Abdul Kalam was born in the Dhanushkodi village of Rameswaram which is now located in Tamil Nadu.
  • He belonged to a poor family and used to sell newspapers to complete his studies.
  • Dr. A. P.J. Abdul Kalam completed his post-graduation in space science from the Madras Institute of Technology.
  • After completing his studies, Dr. A. P.J. Abdul Kalam started working as an aerospace engineer in the Defense Research and Development Organization (DRDO).
  • Dr. A. P.J. Abdul Kalam Joined ISRO (Indian Space Research Organization) in 1962 and got involved in satellite launch activities.
  • Dr. A. P.J. Abdul Kalam was also the President of India from 2002 to 2007.
  • Abdul Kalam died on 27 July 2015 during a lecture in Shillong Meghalaya.
